How to use

1. Tap buttons to build your polynomial: x x² − 1 → x² − 1

2. Tap an operation like Factor or Roots

3. Copy the result for your homework

Operations

  • Factor — x² − 1 → (x−1)(x+1)
  • Expand — (x+1)² → x² + 2x + 1
  • Roots — Where polynomial = 0
  • GCD — Common factor of two polynomials
  • d/dx — Derivative

Number types

  • ℚ — Fractions (default, use for most homework)
  • ℤ — Integers only
  • ℤ₂, ℤ₃... — Modular arithmetic
